Fees & Medicare

Clear, honest, and often partly subsidised

We believe cost shouldn't stand in the way of getting support. With a Mental Health Treatment Plan from your GP, Medicare gives you money back on every session.

  • Claim $145.25 back per session with a clinical psychologist, for up to 10 sessions a year, with a GP Mental Health Treatment Plan.
  • Medicare online claiming — your rebate is processed immediately after you pay.
  • Not sure how it works? Just ask your GP for a Mental Health Treatment Plan — and we'll guide you the rest of the way.
