Our team works with a broad range of mental health concerns, using proven therapies adapted to each person’s age, needs and goals.

Adults

Practical, compassionate therapy to understand patterns, ease distress and build a life that feels more manageable and meaningful.

What happens next

Reaching out can be simple.

You do not need to have everything worked out before you call. Our practice team will listen, answer your questions and help you find the next step.

  1. 1
    Call or send an enquiry

    Tell us who the appointment is for and anything that matters when choosing a clinician.

  2. 2
    We help find the right fit

    We consider your needs, preferences, availability and the clinician’s areas of experience.

  3. 3
    Meet at your pace

    Your first session is a conversation to understand what is happening and discuss how therapy may help.
